Additionally, the box includes lots of basic components:

- 50x LEDs
- 1x RGB led
- 2x photoresistors
- 1x thermistor
- 5x diode rectifiers
- 5x NPN transistor
- 1x IC 4N35
- 1x IC 74HC595
- 10x buttons
- 2x buzzers (active and passive)
- 1x potentiometer
- 10x 22pf c. caps
- 10x 104 c. caps
- 5x e. caps 10uf
- 5x e caps 100uf
- 100x resistors (5x of each: 10R, 100R, 220R, 330R, 1K, 2K, 5K, 10K, 100K, 1M, pin headers).
